# Supplier Intelligence Dashboard: Product Overview

The Supplier Intelligence Dashboard is a web-based supplier relationship management platform designed to help procurement teams make faster, better-informed supplier decisions. It solves a common operational challenge: supplier performance, risk, compliance, and sustainability signals are often spread across disconnected spreadsheets, emails, and audits. This delays action, weakens supplier visibility, and increases the chance of avoidable disruption.

This product gives procurement leaders and supply chain managers one place to see supplier health, understand material-level exposure, and act on exceptions before they become service, quality, or continuity failures.

## Who It Is For

- Procurement managers responsible for supplier governance
- Category managers balancing cost, risk, and service
- Supply chain managers monitoring continuity and single-source exposure
- Cross-functional stakeholders who need a consistent supplier risk view

## How Scoring Works

Each supplier is evaluated across four dimensions to provide a balanced view of operational and strategic health:

- **Performance**: Measures delivery reliability and execution quality (for example on-time delivery, fill rate, defect performance, invoice accuracy).
- **Risk**: Measures structural exposure (for example financial strength, concentration risk, and source dependency).
- **Compliance**: Measures control and audit readiness (for example audit results, certifications, corrective actions, regulatory incidents).
- **Sustainability**: Measures environmental and social responsibility readiness (for example emissions data availability, renewable usage, labor certifications, waste commitments).

These dimensions combine into an **Overall Health Score** for each supplier.

## Material Health Score

The **Material Health Score** represents the supply health of a specific material by combining the health of all suppliers that fulfill that material. This gives teams a practical way to prioritize where supply continuity risk is highest, especially when a material relies heavily on lower-performing suppliers or has single-source dependency.

## Exceptions & Actions

The Exceptions & Actions capability continuously surfaces high-priority risks based on defined business rules, such as supplier risk thresholds, missing compliance evidence, contract renewal exposure, and single-source conditions. Instead of forcing users to inspect every supplier manually, the platform highlights what needs attention now, explains why it triggered, and supports direct supplier notification workflows.

This shortens time-to-value by turning monitoring into action: teams can identify issue concentration, coordinate corrective actions, and maintain supplier accountability with clear next steps.

## Outcomes Delivered

- Improved supplier visibility across performance, risk, compliance, and sustainability
- Earlier risk detection and reduced supply continuity exposure
- Faster triage of corrective actions and contract renewal priorities
- Better decision quality through consistent, transparent scoring
- More confident stakeholder communication through shared metrics

## Scope

### What It Does

- Centralizes supplier and material health intelligence
- Supports configurable weighting to reflect business priorities
- Surfaces rule-driven exceptions with action context
- Enables supplier outreach workflows from within the platform

### What It Does Not Do

- It is not a transactional ERP or procurement execution system
- It does not replace contract authoring or legal workflow tools
- It does not automatically remediate supplier issues without human decision-making
